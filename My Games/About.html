Simon-Game

I learnt this Game from online teaching platform (Udemy). As a beginner i faced big issues in making this game. It was a big challenge for me to play the sounds while the buttons get clicked and to hover the buttons at the same time. Hope you enjoyed this game if you don't know how to play go to "Simon-Game" through short-cut from side bar and then click on the button "How to Play" from the side bar available there.
